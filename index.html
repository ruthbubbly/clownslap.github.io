In short…
Levinkulma is a fully equipped holiday home in a peaceful location at Levijärvi, next to the beautiful Lapland nature and also cross country ski tracks. Only three kilometers to Levikeskus and the downhill slopes, with a Skibus stop a few hundred meters away. The holiday home has three bedrooms and an open space with a total of eight separate beds.

The living room is spacious and high with a beautiful Lapland stone fireplace (more firewood in the shed outside are included in the price)! Fully equipped modern kitchen, with a sauna, drying cupboard and washing machine. Two toilets, two televisions, a home theatre system with blueray for a rainy or cold day. And plenty of space outside for the children to play in the snow!
